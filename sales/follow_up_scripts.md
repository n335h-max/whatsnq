# 🎤 Follow-up Scripts (Day 1 / Day 2 / Day 3)

Most sales happen in the follow-up. Use these if they didn't reply to your first message.

---

### **Day 1: The "Busy?" Follow-up**
"Hi [Name], just checking if you saw my message above? I know running a [Niche] is super busy. 

Reason I'm asking is because I'm only setting up 3 of these AI Bots this week at the RM69 price. Would love for you to be one of them. Let me know if you want to see a quick demo! 👍"

---

### **Day 2: The "Social Proof" Follow-up**
"Hey [Name]! Just set up a similar AI bot for another business today. They’re already seeing faster replies for their customers.

Wanted to make sure you don't miss out on the early-bird price (RM69 lifetime) before it goes up to RM199. Shall I send you the demo screenshots?"

---

### **Day 3: The "Closing the File" (Takeaway)**
"Hi [Name], I haven't heard back so I assume you're not looking for a WhatsApp automation solution right now. No problem at all!

I'll pass the RM69 promo slot to the next person on my list. If you change your mind in the future, feel free to reach out, but the price might be different. 

Wish you all the best with [Business Name]! 🙏"

---

### **Pro Tip:**
- **Voice Notes**: If they haven't replied to text, send the Day 2 message as a quick 20-second voice note. It feels much more personal.
- **Urgency**: Always mention the "3 slots per week" or the "48-hour deadline" to push them to decide.
